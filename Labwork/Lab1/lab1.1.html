Title of Article about having pets during Covid

Author: Yadanar Theint

In my home country, Covid Pandemic was started in March,2021. Since before that, I was already had a cat called Gar Garr. Covid let us to be locked down in our homes for several months and graually we felt abit lonely and started to have a pet like cat, dog or parrot to relief our boring. After some time, I found one stray kitten which was found so poorly and I decided to carry her to my home and named Kook Kook.

    There are several benefits of having them with us.
  • Companionship
  • Help to improve better mental health
  • joy and happiness.
